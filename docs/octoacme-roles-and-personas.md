# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Technical Leads

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide architectural guidance, technical decision-making authority, and oversee technical health and risk mitigation. They bridge product requirements with engineering constraints and long-term system sustainability.

### Responsibilities
- Make and document key technical architecture decisions
- Review and approve technical design documents
- Identify technical risks, dependencies, and integration concerns
- Mentor developers on best practices and code quality standards
- Estimate technical complexity and feasibility assessment
- Participate in capacity planning and technical debt management

### Goals
- Ensure technical solutions are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with long-term strategy
- Minimize technical debt while balancing delivery speed
- Build team capability and technical excellence

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Engineering planning and estimation sessions
- Risk escalations on technical blockers
- Code review guidance and mentorship

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With Developers**: Provides technical direction, reviews designs, removes technical blockers
- **With Product Managers**: Translates technical constraints into scope/timeline trade-offs
- **With Project Managers**: Flags technical risks and provides realistic effort estimates
- **With QA Leads**: Collaborates on test strategy and quality gates

---

## QA/Testing Leads

### Role Summary
QA/Testing Leads own quality assurance strategy, test planning, and acceptance validation. They ensure delivered features meet acceptance criteria and maintain product quality standards throughout the delivery cycle.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and execute test plans aligned with feature requirements
- Define acceptance criteria validation approach
- Identify and report defects with clear reproduction steps
- Manage testing tooling and automation strategy
- Participate in release readiness assessments
- Advocate for quality and usability standards

### Goals
- Deliver features that meet acceptance criteria and user expectations
- Catch defects early and minimize production incidents
- Build efficient testing processes and reduce manual effort through automation

### Typical Communication
- Test planning and strategy discussions
- Defect reports and acceptance validation results
- Sprint reviews and quality metrics
- Release readiness assessments

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With Developers**: Provides test scenarios, validates fixes, collaborates on automation
- **With Product Managers**: Clarifies acceptance criteria and validates feature completeness
- **With Project Managers**: Reports quality status and identifies quality-related risks
- **With Technical Leads**: Discusses test strategy and technical testing challenges

---

## Stakeholder/Sponsor Representatives

### Role Summary
Stakeholder/Sponsor Representatives ensure alignment between project delivery and business objectives. They communicate business priorities, provide decision authority on trade-offs, and ensure stakeholder expectations are managed throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Represent business or stakeholder priorities and constraints
- Provide decision authority on scope, timeline, and resource trade-offs
- Participate in project initiation and success metrics definition
- Review and approve major project decisions and risk mitigations
- Communicate project status and outcomes to broader stakeholder groups
- Escalate unresolved issues to executive sponsors when needed

### Goals
- Ensure project delivers measurable business value
- Maintain stakeholder confidence and alignment throughout delivery
- Reduce scope creep and unplanned work through clear decision-making

### Typical Communication
- Project initiation and kickoff meetings
- Monthly/milestone stakeholder updates
- Risk and issue escalation review
- Go/no-go decision checkpoints

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With Project Managers**: Participates in planning, reviews progress, provides decision authority
- **With Product Managers**: Aligns on success metrics and prioritization rationale
- **With Development Team**: Reviews demos and provides feedback on deliverables

---

## Support/Operations Leads

### Role Summary
Support/Operations Leads ensure delivered features are operational, maintainable, and supported effectively post-release. They represent operational and support perspectives in planning and provide guidance on observability, runbooks, and support readiness.

### Responsibilities
- Identify operational and support implications of features
- Develop runbooks and operational procedures
- Define observability requirements (logging, metrics, alerting)
- Participate in release planning and deployment readiness
- Plan knowledge transfer and support team training
- Participate in post-incident reviews and operational improvements

### Goals
- Ensure features are operationally sound and easily supported
- Reduce operational toil and incident response time
- Enable support team to effectively assist customers

### Typical Communication
- Operational requirements gathering during planning
- Runbook development and release readiness reviews
- Post-deployment verification and incident response
- Support team enablement and knowledge transfer

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With Developers**: Advises on observability, operability, and support considerations
- **With Project Managers**: Identifies operational risks and support readiness requirements
- **With Product Managers**: Communicates support complexity and customer-facing implications
- **With QA Leads**: Collaborates on release readiness and production validation
